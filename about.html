About Jack In The Shop

The story of how craftsmanship, creativity, and a love for tradition came together.

At Jack In The Shop, every piece starts with a story. What began as a small passion project in my workshop has grown into a full-time craft, creating custom laser-engraved wood and metal art for customers all over the country.

I blend traditional woodworking skills with modern laser technology, giving each item both heart and precision. From unique home décor to personalized gifts, my goal is to create something you’ll be proud to display or give.

Every product is handcrafted here in North Carolina, using carefully selected hardwoods and durable metals that stand the test of time. I believe in making things the right way, built to last, with attention to detail that’s hard to find in mass-produced goods.

Whether you’re looking for a one-of-a-kind gift or a custom creation for your own collection, I’m here to bring your ideas to life. Thank you for supporting small business and American craftsmanship.
